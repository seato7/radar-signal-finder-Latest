# Enterprise Custom Integrations

## What's Included

Enterprise plan includes custom integrations tailored to your specific needs:

### Available Custom Integrations

1. **Custom Data Feeds**
   - Integration with proprietary data sources
   - Real-time custom market data ingestion
   - Alternative data provider connections

2. **Custom Broker Integrations**
   - Support for brokers not in standard list
   - Private/institutional broker API connections
   - Multi-broker aggregation workflows

3. **Custom Alert Channels**
   - Direct integration with your Slack workspace
   - Microsoft Teams notifications
   - Custom webhook endpoints
   - SMS alerts via your provider

4. **Custom Reporting & Analytics**
   - Integration with your BI tools (Tableau, PowerBI, etc.)
   - Custom data exports to your data warehouse
   - Automated report generation to your specifications

5. **Custom Strategy Deployment**
   - Deploy your proprietary trading algorithms
   - Private strategy backtesting infrastructure
   - Custom risk management rules

6. **System Integration**
   - SSO/SAML authentication with your identity provider
   - API integration with your internal systems
   - Custom audit logging to your compliance systems
